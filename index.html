About Me

Hey there! I'm Mukesh, an undegraduate student currently pursuing a bachelor's degree in Biotechnology at NIT Warangal. I mainly experiment with building financial tools on crypto rails, deep learning, computational biology, and game emulators.

I'm currently working on Cleopetra, where we're building a new financial instrument called the Thesis Index, which allows anyone with a macro view of the world to become a fund manager. I'm also currently working at Saket Lab as an undergraduate research intern, where I'm contributing to Seqout, a search engine for sequence datasets.

I've previously worked on CandyPay, a seamless checkout solution built on top of Solana Pay, where I was responsible for building the core backend infrastructure. It was used by 500+ ecosystem teams, including Solana Foundation and Prospera Global, and was also used by Balaji Srinivasan during one of his Network State meetups.

Besides the technical stuff, I'm also a bit of a fan of photography. I love watching movies, especially crime thrillers, science fiction and medical drama. And of course, I LOVE CATS! I mostly hang out online on Discord with a bunch of my friends, so feel free to hop in :D
