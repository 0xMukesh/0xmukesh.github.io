Hey there! It has been a while since I've written any technical blog. I've recently got a bit curious about inference engines of LLMs and wanted to build a tiny Llama 2 inference engine in Go. I thought of going through attention mechanism as a refresher before starting the project. This blog posts contains the notes which I made during those refresher sessions.

Attention is all you need paper was the turning point in modern era deep learning. The multi-head attention mechanism and transformer architecture which was introducted in the paper was initially proposed as sequence transduction model but after ~5 years, the architecture is used in almost every aspect of deep learning.
